Cerebellum

Skeletons are approaching you! Answer maths questions before they reach you.

I wanted to learn a party trick where you can mentally cube root a number from 1 - 100 and I then made a text editor version. Wanting to learn more about programming and to creatively express myself I made this game. I created everything but the background music in a couple days.

What I learned:

Even though this was my first software project, in the future I will plan out the architecture rather than starting out with the easiest part of the code. This allowed me to not encounter as much bugs as I did on my first software project. I also learnt my way around the Unity game engine ecosystem.

Instructions:

Before the skeleton attacks you, you must answer a mathematical question regarding cube roots. Below the game screen there is a cubed number, find it's cube root. Depending on where the skeleton is you will do one of the following: if it's in melee range you swing your magical sword, if not you will throw your shield. Play until you die. Good Luck!
